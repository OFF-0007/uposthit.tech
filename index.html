How UPosthit Works

A frictionless experience for both employees and administrators.

Step 1 • Employee App

Arrive & Snap

Employees open the UPosthit mobile app upon arriving at the site. They are prompted to take a live selfie which corresponds to their registered facial profile.

Liveness Detection Fast Processing
Step 2 • Background Sync

Location Validation

The app simultaneously fetches the user's GPS coordinates. If they are within the geofence perimeter of their assigned worksite, the attendance is verified and pushed to the cloud.

Step 3 • Admin Portal

Automated Salary Calculation

At the end of the month, the admin opens the dashboard. The system has already calculated total present days, late penalties, and generated compliant payroll slips ready for disbursement.
